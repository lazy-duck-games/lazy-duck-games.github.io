Disdice

Overview

The main character is a 6-sided die that has to fight the other types of dice: the D4s, D10s and D20s. All this to assert his supremacy, because now the other types of dice seem to be taking over. There is no real ending: the goal is to survive as much as possible, as the dice increase, and to score the best score. You have at your disposal six weapons given by the number of faces that come up immediately after activating the "SHAKE" function. Each weapon is held by a different dice variant.

Controls

• WASD: Move
• Mouse: Aim
• Left click: Fire
• Spacebar: Shake
